**Disney+ Hotstar (HTSR)**

Disney+ Hotstar is a subscription streaming service owned by JioStar, originally launched in 2015 as Hotstar. Following Disney's acquisition of 21st Century Fox in 2019, it was rebranded as Disney+ Hotstar in April 2020, adding content from Disney, Pixar, Marvel, Lucasfilm, and National Geographic. It operates in India, Indonesia, Malaysia, and Thailand, combining local content with the Disney+ library, and is the second largest streaming platform in the world by subscribers, behind only Netflix.

For more information, visit the [Disney+ Hotstar Wikipedia page](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Disney%2B_Hotstar){:target="_blank" rel="noopener noreferrer"}.
