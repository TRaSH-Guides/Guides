**HDR**<br>

This Custom Format serves as a catch-all for all HDR-related formats, including those with `HDR10` or `HDR10+` fallback capabilities, such as `DV HDR10` or `DV HDR10+`.

**Supported Formats**

- `HDR10+`
- `HDR10`
- `PQ`
- `HLG`
