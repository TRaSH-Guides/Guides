**Line/Mic Dubbed**

A Line/Mic Dubbed release is a recording where the audio has been captured from a cinema screening, either through a microphone placed in the theater or via a direct line connection to the audio system. The audio quality is generally poor compared to official releases, as it may include background noise, audience sounds, or distortion. This type of audio is commonly associated with early cam or screener releases and is considered one of the lowest quality audio sources available.
