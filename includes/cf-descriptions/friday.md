**friDay Video**

friDay Video is a Taiwanese streaming service owned by Far EasTone, one of Taiwan's major telecom providers. It is Taiwan's number one domestic streaming platform, offering movies, dramas, variety shows, live news, and anime. It is especially popular for Korean, Japanese, and local Taiwanese content.
