### cross-seed

[cross-seed](https://www.cross-seed.org/){:target="\_blank" rel="noopener noreferrer"} automatically finds and adds cross-seedable torrents across your indexers, so a single piece of media can seed to multiple trackers without duplicating the underlying files on disk.

- Matches against your existing library and injects matching torrents directly into your torrent client.
- Supports both on-demand and continuous (RSS/announce-driven) matching.
- Respects the guide's shared folder structure — matched torrents point at the same data instead of triggering a re-download.
